## Slide 2: Problem
**Mobile dog groomers run their businesses with:**
- Google Calendar (no deposit protection)
- Text messages (manual reminders)
- Venmo/Cash (no receipts)
- Spreadsheets (lost data)

**Result:**
- 10-15% no-show rates
- Double-bookings
- 10+ hours/week on admin

---

## Slide 3: Solution
**Groomly: All-in-one platform for mobile groomers**

- Online booking 24/7 with deposits
- Route optimization for van-based scheduling
- Customer CRM with pet profiles
- Automated SMS/email reminders
- Secure payment processing

**Outcome:**
- 80% fewer no-shows
- Professional customer experience
- Scalable operations
